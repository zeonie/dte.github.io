Belarus Pavilion at Gulfood 2021

Gulfood 2021

The year’s first live, in-person key annual food & beverage trade show − Gulfood 2021 features 20 halls packed with diverse products from 85 country pavilions. Powerful business matchmaking programme unlocks productive, lucrative discussions between exhibitors and buyers.

The Belarus Pavilion presents twenty five of the country’s major manufacturers of dairy and meat products, poultry and eggs, sweets, grocery and cereals. All of them have Halal and ISO certificates.


The delegation is headed by:

Vladimir Ulakhovich

the Chairman of the Belarusian Chamber of Commerce and Industry

Igor Brylo

the Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Food of the Republic of Belarus

Alexander Yakovchits
