About company

Molochny Mir is among the leaders of the dairy industry in the Republic of Belarus. Modern enterprise management systems comprise the foundation making it possible to keep the leading positions and to process over 500 thousand tons of milk annually.

Key points:

  • one of the leaders of the dairy industry in Belarus
  • more than 2,000 qualified employees
  • 7 production sites
  • modern standards of enterprise management
  • more than 360 tons of delicious and quality products every day
  • more than 300 items under the brand Molochnyi Mir.

Key products

Sterilized milk

Sterilized milk is made of natural cow milk, without any preservatives added. The special high temperature treatment method and sealed packing ensure product’s high quality and its perfect natural taste.

See more...

Sterilized cream

Drinking cream is the product easily digestible, with high biological value of major components: protein, milk fat, vitamins and mineral salts. It is made of whole milk, without any preservatives added.

See more...

Unsalted sweet cream butter

In terms of its composition and taste, it resembles home-made butter from farmers. This is classical cream butter. Cream butter is widely used to cook various dishes, significantly improving their nutritive value and taste. This butter is made of fresh high-quality cream, it has delicate taste and contains the set of necessary vitamins and useful substances.

See more...

Cheese

Сheeses of Molochny Mir are the real gold of Belarus. As one of the largest cheesemaking enterprises in the country, we are happy to share our achievements and successes with every gourmet of this noble product. The high quality of our cheeses is confirmed by more than 150 awards from republican and international competitions. Three modern workshops, experienced cheese makers and love for their work. This is what gives our cheeses such a juicy aroma and unique taste. We use both global trends and our own technologies to make natural and high-quality cheese. Try it and you will understand what it means to consciously fall in love with CHEESE!
