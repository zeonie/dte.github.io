About the company

Primemilk is a producer of high quality whey-based products with a wide range of application.

Key points:

  • full automation of production process
  • visual monitoring of all the processing stages
  • high-level processing of raw materials with the use of waste-free production processes in the development and production of all types of products
  • production by client's request/receipe
  • 95% of products are exported

Key products

Fat filled whey powder with fat weight content from 20 up to 55 %

Produced from pasteurized lactic liquid whey and vegetable fats, including further heat-treating, condensation, homogenization and drying on spray drier, intended for food use
