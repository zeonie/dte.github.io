About the company

Soligorsk Poultry Factory is one of the leaders in the production of chicken eggs and absolute leader in the production of quail eggs.

Key points:

  • one of the largest manufacturers of egg products in the Republic of Belarus
  • the daily volume of chicken eggs is 1 million 100 thousand pieces, quail eggs - 311 thousand pieces daily
  • the leader in the egg business for new products

Key products

Chicken and quail eggs

Smoked chicken and quail eggs

Marinated chicken and quail eggs

Multicoloured chicken eggs 'Fermerskiye'

Frozen quail meat, smoked quail meat
