About company

    Vitba is the leader of the Belarusian market of breakfast cereals, and one of the largest Belarusian enterprises in the sphere of pastry:
  • high-tech manufacturing with an average annual output more than 11 thousand tons
  • a well-developed system of distribution: selling units in each regional center of Belarus
  • wide geography of exports: Estonia, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Latvia, Moldova, Russia, Ukraine, etc.

Key points:

  • the leader on the breakfast cereals and wafer products markets
  • wide range of products
  • the best equipment and innovative technologies
  • almost 30 years on the marketing

Pillows "Vitba" with cacao, 130g

Pillows Vitba are a multi-grain shell, wheat fiber and soft filling. At the heart of the shell of the pillows are three cereals: rice, wheat, oats.
