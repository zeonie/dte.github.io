About the company

Rogachev Tinned Dairy Industrial Complex (Rogachev TDIC) is one of the largest enterprises of the Republic of Belarus for processing and production of natural dairy products.

Key points:

  • one of the largest enterprises of the Republic of Belarus for processing and production of natural dairy products
  • usage of only natural and environmentally friendly raw materials
  • enterprise capacity (per day): raw milk processing 1 200 tons
  • condensed milk TM «Rogachev» is a natural product made strictly according to GOST and composed of only two ingredients: milk and sugar
  • condensed milk TM «Rogachev» is produced by a unique technology of continuous condensation developed by specialists of the company, whereby the product is obtained with a unique creamy taste.
