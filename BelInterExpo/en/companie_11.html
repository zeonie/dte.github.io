About the company

Slonim Meat Processing Plant – one of the leaders of meat-processing industry in Belarus, specialized in production of meat, sausage, specialty meat products, prefabricated products, canned products and canned foods for cats and dogs.

Key points:

  • one of the leaders of meat-processing industry in Belarus
  • Halal certificate
  • the program «safe product − a healthy nation» functions since 2006
  • exporters and processors of meat products
  • availability of own raw material resources base that ensure provision of healthy Belarusian foods.
