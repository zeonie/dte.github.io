About company

Grodno Meat Processing Plant is one of the largest integrated meat processing companies in Belarus. It has been active in the meat market for over 100 years and specialises in the production and sale of meat, sausages, meat delicacies, and semi-finished products.

Key points:

  • more than 100 years on the market
  • the enterprise conforms to the standards and norms existing today
  • in increasing competition in the market of meat products
  • it manufactures 3 500 tons of meat and by-products, 2 850 tons of cooked meats and successfully sells these volumes on the markets of sale
