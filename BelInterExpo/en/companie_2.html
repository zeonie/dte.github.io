About company

The Belarussian State Concern of Food Industry Belgospishcheprom is the leading food producer. As concern we unite 46 companies, including companies of: sugar, confectionary, vegetable fat and oil, distillery, beer, tobacco and other branches of food industry.

Key points:

  • includes 46 food producers
  • the annual volume of sales effected by the Сoncern’s enterprises exceeds 2 billion EURO per year
  • the main specialization is production of dry milk baby formulas for newborns, specialized products for pregnant women and nursing mothers, follow-up formulas and cereals for healthy children alongside with formulas for children with intolerance for cow's milk, lactose and sucrose
  • annually the concern exports its products to more than 55 countries world wide
