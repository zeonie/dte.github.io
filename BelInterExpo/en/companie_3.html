About company

Volkovysk OJSC «Bellakt» is a well-known brand, backed with high-tech, large-scale production facilities, whose success is grounded in good stewardship and noble traditions worthy of respect. The production site is certified according to ISO 9001, FSSC 22000, HACCP, and HALAL management systems.

Key points:

  • leading dairy processing company and baby food producer in the Republic of Belarus
  • trusted manufacturer with 50-year history
  • the main specialization is production of dry milk baby formulas for newborns, specialized products for pregnant women and nursing mothers, follow-up formulas and cereals for healthy children alongside with formulas for children with intolerance for cow's milk, lactose and sucrose
  • the production capacity of the enterprise allows to process over 700 tons of milk per day
  • up to 40% of all products are exported.
