About company

Babushkina Krynka is one of the leading milk processing company in the Republic of Belarus.

  • more than 40 years on the market
  • the product range includes over 300 products

  • the production capacity of the enterprise allows to process over 2500 tons of milk per day
  • 60% of products are exported
